# Accuracy label

Profile `modern-small`. Alpha release: geometry=scaffold-baseline, politics=scaffold-baseline. Modern open-geodata scaffold is the engineering foundation—not a final claim of historical truth.

## Quality tiers

| Layer | Tier |
| --- | --- |
| Geometry | `scaffold-baseline` |
| Politics | `scaffold-baseline` |

**Geometry:** Modern open-geodata scaffold only. Politics project modern parent_country_id (or coarse demo remaps). Not a claim of historical accuracy for any pre-modern or wartime era.

**Politics:** Modern open-geodata scaffold only. Politics project modern parent_country_id (or coarse demo remaps). Not a claim of historical accuracy for any pre-modern or wartime era.

## Honest statements

- Province polygons come from modern Natural Earth (and optional admin candidates), not from proprietary game maps.
- Scenario ownership is a separate layer; geometry is not rewritten per era in this alpha.
- demo remaps and baseline projection are scaffolding tools, not official era programs (see M12+ for curated eras).
- Redistribute with attribution.json and the release manifest.

## Do not claim

- Paradox-grade historical accuracy
- curated official 1444 / 1836 / 1936 politics
- period-correct province geometry worldwide
- legal maritime boundaries (sea zones are gameplay abstractions)
- complete population fidelity without documented raster inputs
- production-ready historical start-date tags
- era-aware borders for pre-modern or wartime maps

## Scenario notes

- modern-baseline: projects each land province's modern parent_country_id to owner/controller. Scaffold only.
- demo-1444: coarse country/region remaps for tooling demos. Not curated 1444 politics; do not market as an official era.

## Catalog

Official product tiers (progressive fidelity):

- `scaffold-baseline` — Modern open-geodata scaffold only. Politics project modern parent_country_id (or coarse demo remaps). Not a claim of historical accuracy for any pre-modern or wartime era.
- `curated-politics` — Human-reviewed owner/controller/cores/claims for priority regions and/or global major tags. Geometry may still be modern scaffold.
- `period-geometry` — Era-aware province shapes or boundary hints where modern admin outlines would fail a historian or Paradox-eye sniff test.
